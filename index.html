Easy Pesy Pesto

By Ally Jesse

Cook Time:
10 minutes
Serves:
3-4
  • What to buy for this recipe
  • Steps for recipe

Ingredients

  • Package of Penne Pasta
  • 2 cups of basil
  • 6 tbsp of olive oil
  • ½ cup of parmesan
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • ¼ cup of walnuts

Notes about ingredients

  • I love using penne for pesto, but you can use any pasta of your choice.
  • I also like to use pesto for a spread on sandwhiches, as an addition to pasta salads, really anything you want to add it to!

Preparation

  1. Fill a medium to large pot with water and a pinch of salt. Place on stove top on medium heat and wait to boil.
  2. Once the water is boiling, place the penne (or your choose of pasta) in the water and boil for 7-9 minutes.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, in a food processor or blender place the basil, olive oil, parmesan, garlic, and walnuts and blend until well combined, in the texture you want.
  4. Once the pasta is done cooking, place in a bowl and add a spoonful of the pesto on top. Finish with a topping of parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Enjoy!
