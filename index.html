Easy Pesy Pasta

By Ally Jesse

Cook Time:
20 minutes
Serves:
3-4
  • What to buy for this recipe
  • Steps for recipe

Ingredients

  • Package of Linguine
  • White Button Mushrooms
  • Butter
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Lemons, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil

Notes about ingredients

  • I love linguine for this recipe, but you can use any pasta of your choice.
  • The amount of lemon juice and olive oil is a base amount. You can add however much or less to your pasta that you want.

Preperation

  1. Fill a medium to large pot with water and a pinch of salt. Place on stove top on medium heat and wait to boil.
  2. Once the water is boiling, place the Linguine or your choose of pasta in the water.
